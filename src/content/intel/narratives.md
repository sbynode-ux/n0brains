## Narrative Deep Dives

### 1. Privacy Coins — The Breakout Narrative

**Strength: Strong | Trend: Surging ↑**

Zano (ZANO) is the #1 trending coin on CoinGecko despite being ranked #207 by market cap. Zcash (ZEC) at #16 market cap is also trending at #12. This is the widest divergence between market cap rank and search interest of any narrative.

**What's driving it:**
- Regulatory pressure on transparent blockchains
- Growing institutional demand for confidential transactions
- Potential CBDC developments pushing users toward privacy-preserving alternatives
- ZEC's established market cap (#16) gives it legitimacy that newer privacy coins lack

**Risk:** Privacy coins face regulatory headwinds. The Tornado Cash precedent means this narrative could reverse quickly on enforcement news.

**Strength: Strong | Trend: Rising ↑ | Signal PnL: +0.58**

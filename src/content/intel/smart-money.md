## Narrative Analysis

### 1. Privacy Coins — The Dark Horse
Zano (#207 market cap, #1 trending) and Zcash (#16 market cap, #12 trending) are both surging in search interest. This is unusual — privacy coins have been out of favor since Tornado Cash sanctions. The resurgence suggests:
- Regulatory uncertainty driving interest in privacy-preserving assets
- Potential catalyst: upcoming privacy regulations or CBDC developments
- Zcash at #16 market cap with trending interest = institutional curiosity

### 2. AI Infrastructure — Sustained Interest
Bittensor (TAO, #37) and Venice Token (VVV, #90) represent the AI narrative. Unlike the meme coin frenzy of Q1, this is more sustained, fundamentals-driven interest. TAO's market cap rank (#37) combined with trending status suggests genuine accumulation, not just speculation.

### 3. RWA / Tokenization — Ondo Leads
Ondo (ONDO, #47) is the highest-ranked trending coin outside of BTC/SOL/ZEC. The RWA narrative has been building for months, and Ondo's consistent presence in trending lists suggests institutional capital is paying attention to tokenized real-world assets.

### 4. Meme Coins — Speculative Tail
Pepe (PEPE, #53), Pudgy Penguins (PENGU, #96), and Kishu Inu (KISHU, #813) represent the speculative end. KISHU at #813 market cap but #10 trending is a classic low-cap speculation signal — retail chasing moonshots while smart money rotates to BTC.

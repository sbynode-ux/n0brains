This is the first post on n0brains.com.

If you're reading this, the site is live. The domain resolves. The DNS propagated. The deploy pipeline worked.

And none of that required a week of deliberation.

### The philosophy

Most projects die before they ship. Not because they're bad ideas, but because someone decided they needed:

- A proper brand kit
- The perfect tech stack
- Market validation
- A business plan
- Investor approval

None of that matters. What matters is that you **build it** and **put it out there**.

This site was built in an afternoon. Plain HTML, CSS, and Astro on the backend. GitHub Pages for hosting. A domain from Namecheap. That's it.

### No brains required

The name isn't self-deprecation — it's a reminder. You don't need to be the smartest person in the room. You don't need the perfect architecture. You don't need to read five more blog posts before starting.

You just need to ship.

Welcome to n0brains.
